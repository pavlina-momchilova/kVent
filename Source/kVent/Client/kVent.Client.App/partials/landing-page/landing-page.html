About Am-vent Comm.v

We are young Belgian company specialized in the areas of ventilation and air conditioning. Our experience, based upon more than 7 years of professional background on the Belgian market, have contributed for our business culture that provides responsibility, loyalty and guarantees perfectly completed work.

Our projects vary from small to industrial ventilation systems. Currently one of our clients is the company that constructs Lidl in Belgium. If you are interested to work together in the future, let us know. We are working with budget or per hour. We are looking forward to hear from you.

  • Installation of any ventilation and ventilation equipment
  • Large external ventilation machines
  • Industrial ventilation
  • Domestic ventilation
  • CLV

What do we offer? Air conditioning.

  • Installation
  • Repair
  • Maintenance
  • Heat pumps
  • VRV and VRF systems
  • Boilers and floor heating
